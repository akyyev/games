    Russian draughts

    Rules in play

    • Regular pieces move one dark square diagonally forward.
    • If a capture is available, it must be played. Regular pieces can capture forward or backward.
    • After a capture, the same piece must keep capturing while another capture is available.
    • A piece becomes a king as soon as it reaches the far edge, even during a capture chain.
    • Kings move diagonally any distance and capture by jumping one enemy piece to any open square beyond it.
    • Draws happen on repeated positions, king-vs-king, or 2 kings vs 1 king after 10 full moves without a win.
